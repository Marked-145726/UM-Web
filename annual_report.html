Message from the President

A Year of Growth and Resilience

Academic Year 2024–2025 was a year of exceptional achievement for the University of Mindanao. Our community of scholars, educators, and students demonstrated remarkable resilience and dedication despite global and regional challenges.

We successfully maintained our ISO 9001:2015 certification, launched five new graduate programs, expanded scholarship coverage to reach over 8,000 scholars across all campuses, and saw record-breaking performance in multiple licensure examinations. I am proud to present these accomplishments to our Board, our families, and our community.

RL

Dr. Romulo P. Lumakin

University President, UM

A.Y. 2024–2025

Academic & Institutional Highlights

Research

142 Research Papers Published

Faculty and student researchers published 142 peer-reviewed articles in indexed national and international journals, a 28% increase over the previous year.

Licensure

95% Average Licensure Pass Rate

UM examinees achieved a 95% average pass rate across all board and licensure examinations, topping the regional results in CPA, Engineering, and Nursing.

Scholarships

₱380M in Financial Aid

Over 8,240 students received scholarship and financial assistance through government, institutional, and private donor programs.

Programs

5 New Graduate Programs Launched

New master's and doctoral programs launched in Data Science, Environmental Management, Public Administration, Nursing Education, and Business Analytics.

Accreditation

ISO 9001:2015 Re-Certification

UM successfully maintained its ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System certification, validated by an external audit conducted by SGS Philippines.

Community

320+ Extension Projects

Faculty and students conducted 320+ community extension and outreach projects across Region XI, serving over 18,000 community beneficiaries.

Strategic Direction

Looking Ahead — UM 2030

Our five-year strategic plan targets 80,000 enrolled students, 200+ programs, expanded international linkages, and a research-intensive culture that directly addresses Mindanao's development needs.

Annual transparency reports like this one ensure every stakeholder can track our progress and hold us accountable to our commitments.
