About UM

The Premier University
of Mindanao

Founded in 1946, the University of Mindanao is the largest private educational institution in Mindanao. With campuses across the region, UM has been a pillar of academic excellence, nurturing generations of professionals, leaders, and innovators committed to national development.

UM holds ISO 9001:2015 certification and is recognized by CHED, the Philippine Accrediting Association of Schools, Colleges and Universities (PAASCU), and various international accrediting bodies.
