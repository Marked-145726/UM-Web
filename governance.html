Overview

How UM is Governed

The University of Mindanao operates under a shared governance model guided by its charter, the Board of Trustees, and the Office of the President, with faculty and student representation embedded in institutional decision-making.

All governance documents, board resolutions, and institutional policies are made available to the university community in the spirit of transparency and accountability.
