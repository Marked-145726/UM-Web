Governing Body

Board of Trustees

The highest governing authority of the University of Mindanao, providing strategic oversight and fiduciary stewardship in pursuit of UM's mission.

About the Board

Stewards of the UM Mission

The Board of Trustees of the University of Mindanao is constituted under the university's charter and is the supreme policy-making body of the institution. It holds the ultimate legal, financial, and fiduciary responsibility for the university and all its assets.

Trustees are elected from distinguished leaders in education, law, business, and public service. The Board meets quarterly and issues resolutions that guide the direction of the university.

Board Member Directory

Meet the distinguished trustees guiding the University of Mindanao.

GT
Chairman of the Board

Dr. Guillermo P. Torres

Founder & Chairman Emeritus · Trustee since 1972

A pioneering force in Mindanaoan education, Dr. Torres has dedicated over five decades to building UM into the region's premier private university. He holds honorary doctorates from four Philippine universities.

ML

CPA Maria L. Ramos

Vice Chairman · Finance

Certified Public Accountant and former CFO of a major Davao conglomerate. Trustee since 2010.

AN

Dr. Aurora S. Navarro

Academic Affairs Chair

Former CHED Regional Director for Region XI. Advocate for quality assurance in higher education. Trustee since 2015.

RM

Dr. Ricardo B. Mendoza

Student Welfare Chair

Pediatrician and civic leader. Leads the Board's scholarship and student assistance programs. Trustee since 2012.

FC

Engr. Felicidad T. Cruz

Facilities Chair

Licensed Civil Engineer and principal of Cruz & Associates. Overseeing UM's campus expansion projects. Trustee since 2018.

BL

Atty. Benjamin L. Soriano

Trustee at Large

Practicing lawyer and former Davao City Councilor. Provides legal and legislative guidance to the Board. Trustee since 2016.

GV

Dr. Grace V. Magsino

Faculty Representative

Professor of Education and chair of the UM Faculty Association. Advocates for faculty rights and professional development.
